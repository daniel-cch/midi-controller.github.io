Pastel MIDI Pad

Simple easy to use MIDI Pad

About

Pastel MIDI Pad is a simple and easy to use MIDI Pad. It works with any DAW (Logic, Pro Tools, Reaper, among others).

Just connect your phone to your computer, enable MIDI communication, and enjoy.

Pastel MIDI Pad supports the following MIDI messages:

  • Control Change
  • Program Change
  • Note Off
  • Note On
  • Custom MIDI Messages
